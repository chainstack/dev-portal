## Overview

The **Unlimited Node add‑on** lets you turn **any** existing RPC node into a flat‑fee, capacity‑capped powerhouse.

Instead of paying per request, you pick an RPS (requests per second) tier, activate the add‑on, and enjoy predictable monthly costs.

### Available RPS tiers

- 25 RPS
- 100 RPS
- 250 RPS
- 500 RPS
- 1000 RPS

<Info>
  ### Add‑on vs. pay-as-you-go
  
  With Unlimited Node, you **do not need the pay-as-you-go** option for the attached node.
  
  The node **consumes zero plan quota** and never incurs overage.
  
  If you exceed the tier limit, you will simply hit a message that the rate exceeds the limit.
</Info>

## Benefits

- **Fixed monthly bill** — no surprises, no overage math.  
- **Instant node upgrade to Unlimited** — flip the switch on any existing node.  
- **Plan quota preserved** — keep your remaining requests for other nodes, subgraphs, or services.  
- **Growth‑first** — available from the **Growth** plan upward.

## Enable Unlimited Node

<Steps>
  <Step>
    In **Nodes**, open the node you want to upgrade.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click **Add-ons** > **Unlimited Node**.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Pick an RPS tier and click **Apply changes**.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Confirm payment. The add‑on starts within seconds.
  </Step>
</Steps>
