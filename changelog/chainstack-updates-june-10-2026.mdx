**Add-ons**. Higher concurrent stream limits for the [Yellowstone gRPC Geyser plugin](/docs/yellowstone-grpc-geyser-plugin):

* **\$49/month** — now 2 concurrent streams per platform account, up from 1.
* **\$149/month** — now 7 concurrent streams per platform account, up from 5.

All other limits are unchanged. See [Yellowstone gRPC Geyser plugin](/docs/yellowstone-grpc-geyser-plugin) for the full breakdown.
