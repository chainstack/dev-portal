**Subgraphs**. Chainstack Subgraphs is migrating to our partner [Ormi](https://ormilabs.com/).

- Effective immediately: You can no longer create new subgraphs on Chainstack.
- Until February 1, 2026: Existing subgraphs will continue to run.
- After February 1, 2026: The Chainstack Subgraphs service will be disabled.
