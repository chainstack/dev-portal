**Protocols.** Hyperliquid Mainnet and Hyperliquid Testnet are now available in full mode with the HyperCore `/info` and HyperEVM `/evm` APIs enabled. See also [Hyperliquid API Reference](/reference/hyperliquid-getting-started). As a reminder, HyperCore is also supported.
